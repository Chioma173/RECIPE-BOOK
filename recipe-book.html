Nigerian Jollof Rice

Jollof Rice is one of the most famous dishes in West Africa. It is cooked with rice, tomatoes, peppers, onions and aromatic spices to create a rich, smoky flavour. Jollof Rice is commonly served at weddings, birthdays, Christmas celebrations, and family gatherings. It is a meal that brings people together and is often considered the pride of Nigerian cuisine.

Ingredient

Ingredient Quantity
Rice 4 cups
Tomatoes 5 Large
Red Bell Pepper 3
Onions 1 big
Vegetable Oil 1 cup
seasoning cube 3
Salt To taste

Cooking Steps

  1. Wash the rice thoroughly.
  2. Blend the tomatoes, peppers and onions.
  3. Heat oil and fry the blended mixture.
  4. Add seasoning, spices and stock.
  5. Add the washed rice and stir well.
  6. Cook until the rice becomes soft.
  7. Serve hot with chicken, fish or salad.
